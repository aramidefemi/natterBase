About Us

CTOO Management

We are experts at managing software development teams and processes. Technologists frequently speak and communicate differently from the business leaders. As your fractional CTO, we can effectively bridge that gap and manage the development resources freeing you up to focus on the business aspects of the company.

Why CTOO?

Most start-ups and midsized companies do not need an experienced full time Chief Technology Officer (CTO). Rather, someone to manage the software developers, the software development life cycle, and partner with the management team to establish short and long term technology strategies. We offer part-time, contract, and interim CTO services.
